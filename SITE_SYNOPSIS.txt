MODULE: PUF-mobile
ROLE: Android cab GPS guidance & field setup
STATUS: active (field tablet app)

ONE-LINER
Qt/Android heading-up guidance app for cab tablets — boundaries, AB lines, coverage recording, and John Deere position via UDP NMEA bridge.

SYNOPSIS
- Cab-side map UI: parses NMEA ($GGA/$RMC/$VTG/$HDT) from UDP bridge, serial, Bluetooth, or tablet GNSS; heading-up tractor/implement, worked-area swaths, job recording as GeoJSON.
- Client → Farm → Field hierarchy persisted locally (ISOXML TASKDATA.XML); import unzipped Ops Center exports or KML from tablet Download/Farm_data/.
- GPS backends: UDP :9999 (recommended for JD StarFire/616R via laptop gps_bridge.exe), internal serial, Bluetooth, tablet GNSS, on-tablet USB-CAN (power-limited).
- Implement setup: width, offset, section count/numbering (JD L/C/R labels) — layout convention mirrored read-only by PUFworks-vision for boom mapping.
- Does NOT run vision inference or ISOBUS section actuation; sprayer control stays on PUFworks-isobus (+ optional PUFworks-vision bitmap feed).
- Workshop builds: dual-ABI APK (armeabi-v7a + arm64), Qt 5.15, minSdk 23; Windows-first local builds, no auto-updater.
